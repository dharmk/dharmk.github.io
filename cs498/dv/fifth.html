On this dashboard we are trying to find out the most profitable sectors and top/bottom 10 companies across each.

Sector is a parameter for the top 10 and bottom 10 companies charts. Click on any of the sectors to select it and trigger the update of top and bottom 10 companies in that sector.

Legends are not added on these charts yet, make use of the tooltips to get more Information. Each color represents an individual sector.

Selected sector is highlighted with a thick stroke around it.

Can you figure out the company with the best and the worst profit across all sectors?
