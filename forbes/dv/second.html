Lets take a look at all the metrics along with the rank in a parallel-coordinates chart. A parallel coordinate chart makes it easier to look at multi-dimensional data.

You can use your mouse to brush along one or more axes to explore the data. To clear any active brush, just click above/below the brush area on the same axis.

If you select companies with higher rank (smaller values), you will notice that they tend to have higher values for most of the metrics.
