Lets take a look at the profitability and profit variance across various sectors. The label below the chart shows the sector name.

Note that Information Technology is the sector with the highest margin and Utilities with the lowest.

Also Financials seem to be the one with the highest variance in profits. There are couple of companies in this sector with high profit margins too.

Apple makes the highest profit (45%) and Unicredit is the one with the highest loss (13%).

Utilities and Materials sector has profit ranges limited to (0-5)%.
