Forbes 500 : Summary

Prev Home

We explored the forbes 500 list with respect to their profit ranges. Major observations include:

1. There is a lot of variance in profitability across top 500 companies.

2. More than 75% of companies have profits in the range of (0-5)%.

3. Some companies are also in loss and the loss is even upto the range of 13% but they have made it to the top 500 list because of rating higher in the other three measures.

4. Apple has the highest profit margin across all sectors (45.2%) and Unicredit Group (Financials) has suffered the biggest loss (13%) among top 500.

5. IT is the best sector in terms of profit margins across top 500 companies.

6. Financials has the most variance in the profit ranges across all companies in this sector.
