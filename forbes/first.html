Lets take a look at the various metrics for each company in the top 500 list.

On this page we are plotting the various metrics like Market Value, Assets, Sales and Profits for each company along the Y-axis. The companies are sorted along the x-axis according to its rank.

In general the overall values seem correlated with the company rank giving a hint that the scores for each of the category are combined in a linear fashion to arrive at the final rank.

Although we see some spikes for some attributes for some companies ranked slightly lower but thats because they didn't fair well in the remaining categories
